ABOUT US

IEEE Electron Devices Society (EDS) is a specialized society within the IEEE that focuses on the advancement of electron devices, semiconductor technology and their applications. Establised in 1954 IEEE EDS promotes the advancement of theory, design, fabrication, manufacturing, reliability, modeling and applications of electron and ion devices.

IEEE EDS IEM Student Chapter focuses on spreading knowledge and awareness related to the technologycal industry such as VLSI design, Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IoT), ect. We as a society focus not only on the technical aspects but also on the soft skills required to achieve greater success in our careers.
